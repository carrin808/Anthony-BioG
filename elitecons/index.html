We are biofuels manufacturer in Nairobi, Kenya, with a focus on environmental conservation and sustainable energy. We repurpose biomass waste, such as timber, coffee husks, and agricultural waste, into biofuels for household and industrial use. This initiative aims to reduce reliance on firewood and charcoal, which are major contributors to deforestation and environmental degradation in Africa. With a significant portion of the population in sub-Saharan Africa dependent on biomass energy, our approach aligns with addressing both environmental and energy challenges in the region. In Kenya specifically, biomass accounts for the majority of energy supply, highlighting the importance of alternative, cleaner energy sources like biofuels.

ABOUT US

Sustainability at the Core of Our Operations

We are biofuels manufacturer in Nairobi, Kenya, with a focus on environmental conservation and sustainable energy. We repurpose biomass waste, such as timber, coffee husks, and agricultural waste, into biofuels for household and industrial use. This initiative aims to reduce reliance on firewood and charcoal, which are major contributors to deforestation and environmental degradation in Africa. With a significant portion of the population in sub-Saharan Africa dependent on biomass energy, our approach aligns with addressing both environmental and energy challenges in the region. In Kenya specifically, biomass accounts for the majority of energy supply, highlighting the importance of alternative, cleaner energy sources like biofuels.

What is Bio-Gold Energy?

Bio-Gold is a pioneering biofuels company based in Nairobi, Kenya, dedicated to environmental conservation and sustainable energy. They specialize in repurposing biomass waste into eco-friendly products such as charcoal briquettes and wood pellets, offering cost-effective alternatives to traditional fuels while championing excellence in quality and sustainability.
